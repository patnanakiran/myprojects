ABOUT US

The Institute of Technical Education and Research is the Engineering College of the Siksha O Anusandhan University situated in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, India. This institute was established in the year 1996 by Dr. Manoj Ranjan Nayak. ITER is the Faculty of Engineering and Technology of Siksha 'O' Anusandhan University. It is located in Campus 1 of the University. ITER is the oldest constituent institute of the University, having seen light in 1997. It has grown from a fledgling campus with a single three storied building (Academic Block) to one of the best engineering campuses in Eastern India.
